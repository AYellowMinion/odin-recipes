It is with great pleasure to share a neat drink that can be served hot or cold.

It probably existed when the British invaded China (something like that) - Milk Tea

A classic take on this dish would be a Hong Kong Styled Milk Tea which emphasizes the simpleness of milk and tea. In as little as 2 ingredients, this drink is a solid foundation and favorite for anyone who wants to keep things simple.
