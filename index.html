Signatue Recipe

It is with great pleasure to share a neat drink that can be served hot or cold.

Probably existed when the British invaded China (something like that) - Milk Tea

The simplest iteration of this drink in my opinion would be made with tea and condensed milk (boba optional).

Ingredients List

What you'll need

  • Tea of Choice (Black preferred)
  • Can of condensed milk (Note: Any can is fine but there are taste differences between brands)
  • Tapioca Pearls (They come dry and are in an vaccumn sealed bag)
  • Honey (Agave or brown rice syrup can work)
  • Ice (Optional)

Equipment List

  • 8 Cup Bowl (I use a glass pyrex mixing bowl, a lid would also be great but plastic wrap or a plate can work in a pinch)
  • Large Spoon or whisk
  • Stove top pot (Boiling your tapioca pearls, adjust based on serving size)
  • Separate Bowl to hold your boba (Anything works, your boba will be sitting in a honey-water mixture until served)
